Who are we?

STAR is a Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) chapter that gives passionate space explorers the resources they need to explore space now instead of waiting until later. We build space exploring hardware and software, go to conventions/competitions, keep tabs on space politics and business, invite knowledgeable guest speakers, and connect our members to the professional world.
