Who are we?

The STAR CubeSat team aims to send Rutgers' first student-built satellite into space. The Sloshing Platform for In-Orbit Controller Experimentation satellite (SPICEsat) will investigate fuel sloshing dynamics in zero-gravity. We aim to design, analyze, and build each system from ground up. Students have the opportunity to work with a team on a real mission alongside professors and industry professionals. The SPICEsat team competed in the 11th cycle of the University Nanosatellite Program (UNP). As of 2024, we reached fulfillment of Phase A, which involved developing a mission concept, beginning construction of an engineering model, and completing six design reviews. If you're interested in space systems and learning more about satellites, check out the CubeSat team!
