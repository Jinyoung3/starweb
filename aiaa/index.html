Rutgers AIAA was the first to establish itself as the leading organization for undergraduate students interested in topics related aerospace at Rutgers University, and as been achieving greatness ever since.

2012

RUAutonomous places 7th in AUVSI SUAS competition and receives sportsmanship award.

2016

RUFPV was founded.

2016 - 2017

RUAirborne switches from SAE Aero to DBF, proposal places 5th of 138 teams.

2017

RRPL was founded.

2018

Captain of RUFPV (Lincoln Black) places 7th at the second annual Collegiate Drone Racing National Championship at Purdue University.

Our members include engineers of many disciplines, including mechanical/aerospace, electrical, and computer, as well as many non-engineers such as computer science, physics and business majors that all share a common mindset. We welcome anyone who has an interest in aerospace regardless of major, year, or experience level.
