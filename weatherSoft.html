Summary

The software subteam works on the brain of the weather balloon. We program Arduinos to collect and record data like pressure, temperature, location, and more from various sensors. We also do basic circuitry and soldering to wire up different components and integrate everything into a single sensor suite. There are no prerequisites required to join, and everyone is welcome to take part!
